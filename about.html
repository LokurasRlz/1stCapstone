MENDOTAKU CONVENTION

Mendotaku is a festival of Japanese and anime culture that takes place every year, twice a year, in the City of Mendoza. Convention of the province of Mendoza dedicated to Anime, Manga, Cosplay, and Asian Pop Culture. Where you can enjoy a wide variety of activities, such as the presentation of International Artists, cosplay contests, projections, karaoke, live bands, video tournaments, games, role-playing, different workshops, and many more!!!

Please contact us per email for any further questions about our Mendotaku Convention!
